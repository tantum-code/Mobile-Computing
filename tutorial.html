Loci-Method-Go

Loci-Method-Go is a gamified approach to Flashcard learning. Your goal is to find vocabs on a Map and solve them.

How to play

The main goal is to find a set of randomly palced vocabs/flashcards on a map. You can upload your own Vocabularies (.csv or .txt file) and place them within a certain radius around your location. Your goal is to reach the destinations marked on your map. If you know the right answer, the location-marker will disappear, if not, you must try again. The game is finished once all Locations were found and all vocabs have been solved correctly.

In order to play, navigate to the "Start" page on the Navigation Menu. When on the Start page, start setting up your game by:
  • Load a CSV or txt File into the File-input prompt
  • Enter a search-radius into the Text-input
  • Press the "Place" button and wait for the map to update
You can always re-navigate to the Start page to place more vocabs on your map. If you want to get rid of the Game-menu, you can navigate to the "Map" section on the Nagivation Menu in order to hide the Settings.
