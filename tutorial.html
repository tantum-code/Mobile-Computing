Loci-Method-Go

Loci-Method-Go is a gamified approach to Flashcard learning. You can choose any set of Vocabularies (as long as they are formatted as a .csv or .txt file) and place them within a certain radius around your location. Your goal is to reach the destinations marked on your map. Once you reach a location, a prompt will show a random vocab from your vocab list. If you know the answer to a vocab, the location will disappear, if not, you must try again. The game is finished once all Locations were found and all vocabs have been solved correctly.

How to play

in order to play, navigate to the "Start" page on the Navigation Menu. When on the Start page, start setting up your game by:
  • Load a CSV or txt File into the File-input prompt
  • Enter a search-radius into the Text-input
  • Press the "Place" button and wait for the map to update
You can always re-navigate to the Start page to place more vocabs on your map. If you want to get rid of the Game-menu, you can navigate to the "Map" section on the Nagivation Menu in order to hide the Settings.
